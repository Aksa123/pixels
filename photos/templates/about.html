Empowering Creators

By providing free stock photos Pexels helps millions of creators all over the world to easily create beautiful products and designs.

What is this?

It is a Pexels clone that I made in my spare time. It took around 3 weeks to finish I think, but hey I was busy with work, too! I was thinking of making an ecommerce website for a portofolio project, but it's just too common and complicated, logic-wise. Photo-sharing web apps are not that technically different anyway, and it's simpler, plus Pexels is just fricking good, so that's why I chose it as my project model.
Only primitive tools are used:

  • Django
  • HTML
  • Javascript + MacyJS, ChartJS
  • CSS + Bootstrap.
Social media icons are taken from Flaticon: Intagram, Facebook, and Linkedin (dear artists, thank you so much!).
